The Best Movies According to Cathy

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Seven Sundays

This is my favourite family movie. This is a Filipino film about a family coming together to reconnect and resolve past conflicts after learning about their father's terminal illness..

3 Idiots

An Indian movie that explores the pressure of academic success through the journey of three engineering students who challenge societal expectations and follow their passions.

Taare Zameen Par

An Indian film highlighting the struggles of a dyslexic child and the transformative power of understanding and creativity in education.
